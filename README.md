# Cryptographic Settlement Research

Independent research exploring cryptographic verification of settlement integrity across heterogeneous distributed ledgers.

The research investigates whether settlement states across different networks can be verified cryptographically rather than relying on traditional bridge architectures or intermediaries.

This work is currently at an early conceptual and prototype exploration stage.

Research exploration started: November 2025.

No technical implementation details are disclosed at this time.
